The robustness of (interdependent) systems

Robustness of systems

Property to resist perturbations...

...without service degradation...

...nor repair.

Robustness of interdependent systems

Property to resist perturbations...

...without service degradation...

...nor repair...

...including services/interdependencies between the subsystems

Why is robustness important?

  • Systems that may never fail
  • Minimising risks of unexpected events
  • Avoid unwanted emerging behaviour

There are different levels of robustness of a system.

The overall goal

Maintain the most robust state of an interdependent system anytime.

Required capabities

  • Evaluation of the robustness
    • Measure, quantify, compare(, and predict)
  • Control of the robustness
    • System reconfiguration, environment modification
